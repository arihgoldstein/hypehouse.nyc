"Hye" — Armenian for Armenian

Hye House is an Armenian-led creative community and event series. Born out of a desire to see our culture represented in the spaces we frequent, we bridge heritage with the underground through sound, design, and shared experience. We host events, curate experiences, and build community across Brooklyn, Los Angeles, and Yerevan.
