"Hye" — Armenian for Armenian

Hye House is an Armenian-led creative community and event series. Born out of a desire to see our culture represented in the spaces we frequent, we bridge heritage with the underground through sound, design, and shared experience.

We host DJ nights, cultural gatherings, and creative experiences that bring together the Armenian diaspora and our broader creative community. From warehouse parties in Brooklyn to intimate gatherings in Los Angeles, we create spaces where culture meets the dance floor.

What We Do

We curate events that feel like home—where the music speaks to something deeper, where the crowd is family, and where Armenian identity intersects with global creative culture. Our programming spans electronic music, art installations, and community building.
